Suits

Story:
A legal drama centered on Mike Ross, a brilliant college dropout with a photographic memory who lands a job as an associate for top Manhattan corporate lawyer Harvey Specter, despite never attending law school. Together, they win cases while hiding Mike's secret, navigating firm politics, high-stakes litigation, and personal relationships over nine seasons.
